  <action>
**In `_button.scss`:**
1. `.btn` — `@include text($white, 500, $text-md)` becomes `@include text(var(--color-text-inverse), 500, $text-md)`
2. `.btn--primary` — `background-color: $purple` becomes `background-color: var(--color-accent)`
3. `.btn--secondary` — `border: 1px solid $purple` becomes `border: 1px solid var(--color-accent-border)`, `background-color: $black-900` becomes `background-color: var(--color-surface-secondary)`
4. `.btn:disabled` — `background-color: $gray-500` stays as `$gray-500` (disabled state is intentionally muted across themes, OR use `var(--color-text-light)` for consistency)
5. `border-radius: 99rem` — Keep as-is (pill shape is structural, not themed per the Foretagarna spec which uses 4px for standard buttons but the chatbot's pill buttons are a design choice)

**In `_markdown.scss`:**
1. `.markdown-body` — `color: $white-2` becomes `color: var(--color-bubble-bot-text)` (this is bot message content), `font-size: $text-md` stays as SCSS variable
2. `code` — `font-family: $font-family` becomes `font-family: var(--font-family)`, `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.4)` becomes `background: var(--color-accent-subtle)` (makes inline code use subtle accent highlight)
3. `pre` (code block) — `background: #111218` becomes `background: var(--color-surface-secondary)` (dark code block on both themes, but Foretagarna will get light gray which is acceptable)
4. `th` — `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.04)` becomes `background: var(--color-bg-alt)` (table header background)
5. `th, td` — `border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` becomes `border: 1px solid var(--color-border)`
6. `blockquote` — `border-left: 3px solid #cccccc` becomes `border-left: 3px solid var(--color-border)`, `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` becomes `background: var(--color-bg-alt)`, `color: $white-2` becomes `color: var(--color-text)`
7. `a` — `color: $purple` becomes `color: var(--color-accent)`

**In `_toast.scss`:**
1. `.globy-toast` — `border: 1px solid $purple` becomes `border: 1px solid var(--color-accent-border)`, `background: rgba(252, 244, 255, 1)` becomes `background: var(--color-bg) !important` (white/near-white on both themes)
2. `.globy-toast > div` — `color: $purple` becomes `color: var(--color-accent)`

**In `_spinner-loading.scss`:**
1. `.loading` — `background-color: rgba($color: $black-900, $alpha: 0.5)` becomes `background-color: var(--color-surface-overlay)`
2. `.loader` — `background: $purple` becomes `background: var(--color-accent)`

**In `_typing-indicator.scss`:**
1. `.dot` — `background-color: $purple` becomes `background-color: var(--color-accent)`

**In `_modal.scss`:**
1. `.modal__overlay` — `background: rgba($color: $black-950, $alpha: 0.5)` becomes `background: var(--color-surface-overlay)`

**In `_circular-progress.scss`:**
1. `&::before` conic-gradient — `$white-2` becomes `var(--color-text-heading)`, `$gray-500` becomes `var(--color-text-light)`
2. The `-webkit-mask` and `mask` — `$black-700` becomes `var(--color-surface-primary)`

**In `_common.scss`:**
1. `.thinking .txt` — the `background: linear-gradient($gray-300, $gray-300)` becomes `background: linear-gradient(var(--color-text-muted), var(--color-text-muted))`. The rgba(255,255,255,...) gradient parts stay — these are intentionally white shimmer overlay regardless of theme.

IMPORTANT: Keep `@use "../../abstracts" as *` (or `@use "../../abstracts/" as *`) import in every file. Keep all structural properties unchanged. Only replace COLOR references. Font SIZE SCSS variables ($text-md, etc.) stay as SCSS vars.
